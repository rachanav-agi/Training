Definition and Usage. The :hover selector is used to select elements when you mouse over them. Tip: The :hover selector can be used on all elements, not only on links. Tip: Use the :link selector to style links to unvisited pages, the :visited selector to style links to visited pages, and the :active selector to style the active link.
